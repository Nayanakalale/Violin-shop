Violin Repairs

We have an extensive instrument and bow repair workshop. We offer fast, friendly and professional service for all your violin, viola, cello and bass repair and restoration needs. We give free repair estimates. Custom setups and acoustic adjustments can be made to your violin, viola, cello, or bass to optimize its sound. We give free verbal appraisals and written ones for $35. All major repairs are guaranteed for as long as you own your instrument. Our workshop staff can help you decide whether to undertake an extensive repair or whether it would be a better option to purchase another instrument. So stop by for a consultation with our knowledgeable workshop staff. For more information about violin shop repair and restoration, please feel free to contact us anytime. As a unique resource for you, we have posted the description and prices of some of our most common repairs.

Inventory

Our top priority at The Violin Shop is to provide instruments and bows that will inspire and meet the needs of musicians of all calibers. We search regionally, nationally and internationally, using various resources, to maintain a fine selection of inventory of all prices, sizes and quality to accommodate the specific needs of each player. We seek instruments that are in as close to pure condition as possible, with minimal cracks and play wear. Once an instrument or bow is in our possession we restore, repair and adjust them to achieve their maximum in tone and playability. At The Violin Shop we stock instruments picked by musicians, for musicians.
